Essential Eight Uplift — ML1, ML2, or ML3

The flagship SMB engagement

Essential Eight is the Australian Cyber Security Centre's prioritised mitigation strategy set, assessed across three maturity levels. Cyber by Exegesis delivers uplift engagements at the maturity level that fits your business — ML1 for a baseline you can defend, ML2 for hardening against opportunistic attackers, ML3 for resilience against state-level adversaries.

Each engagement opens with a gap assessment against the named maturity level, produces a prioritised implementation plan, and closes with an evidence pack you can show to a customer, an insurer, or an auditor. We name the level honestly — never blur ML1 against ML2 to claim more than we've delivered.

The waitlist is open for SMBs across Sydney, Melbourne, Brisbane, Perth, and Adelaide. We sequence engagements by tenant type (Microsoft 365 first, Google Workspace second) and sector.

Three families of engagement:

  • Individuals & families — scam triage, MFA setup, identity recovery, family digital hygiene, lost-device response
  • SMB controls uplift — Essential Eight ML1/ML2/ML3, endpoint protection rollout, BEC prevention, backup & ransomware preparedness
  • SMB incident & readiness — Notifiable Data Breach response, cyber insurance readiness review
  • Fixed-scope, fixed-fee, written-report deliverables
  • Australian regulatory anchoring — ACSC, OAIC, ACCC Scamwatch, eSafety
  • Sequenced by tenant + sector for predictable delivery
