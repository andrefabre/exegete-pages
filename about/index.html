ABOUT EXEGESIS

A small Australian company
with a narrow philosophy

Exegesis builds specialised AI agent stacks for regulated Australian industries. Four pillars, one operator, one design philosophy: stay narrow, cite primary sources, and validate buyer demand before building product.

How we work

Primary sources, not marketing

Every regulatory claim on every door traces to a primary public source — ACSC, OAIC, ASIC, Federal Court, ART, ACCC, eSafety. We don't quote "studies show" without showing you the study.

Doors as sensors, not commitments

Each pillar runs ~150 landing pages ("doors") tuned to specific buyer cohorts and problems. Doors test demand. We commit to building a product only when buyers ask for it.

Australian jurisdiction first

Every pillar is scoped to Australian law, Australian regulators, and Australian buyers. We're not generic — we're built for one market and the regulations that govern it.
