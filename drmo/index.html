Digital Risk Management Officer (DRMO) Services

Fractional DRMO support — buy by service

DRMO gives an Australian SMB access to experienced cybersecurity risk management without the cost of a full-time hire. Services are scoped, fixed-fee, and delivered against Australian regulatory frameworks — ACSC Essential Eight, ISO 27001, and the relevant state-level conveyancing and identity-verification rules.

Each DRMO service is bought through a dedicated landing page (a "door") tuned to the buyer cohort, location, and regulatory framework that fits your situation. Doors carry Stripe-hosted checkout — Australian-dollar pricing, email receipt, no account required.

Delivery is via written report and (where relevant) follow-up call. Most services complete in 3–7 business days.

What you get with every DRMO engagement:

  • Written assessment grounded in primary Australian sources (ACSC, OAIC, ACCC)
  • Prioritised remediation list with effort and risk ratings
  • Stripe tax invoice on purchase
  • Fixed-fee, no scope creep, no surprise add-ons
  • Direct delivery by the principal — no junior handoff
  • 30-day clarification window after delivery
