Citation Verification Agent — built on RuleCheck

The flagship Legal service

The Citation Verification Agent extracts every cited authority from a draft submission, queries an Australian authority registry (High Court, Federal Court, Federal Circuit and Family Court, State Supreme Courts, AustLII), and returns each citation with a verification status: verified, mismatched, or not found. No draft content leaves the verification path to an external LLM — the checker is local-first and deterministic.

It is built on RuleCheck, our open-source pre-lodgement checker, and currently runs as a live beta at rulecheck.onrender.com. The Legal pillar wraps RuleCheck with buyer-specific workflows, audit logging, and a structured readiness report tailored to the Federal Court GPN-AI candour duty.

The waitlist is open for solicitors and boutique firms across Sydney, Melbourne, Brisbane, Perth, and Adelaide. Pricing tiers (per-filing, per-user monthly, firm licence) are being shaped by what we hear from the waitlist.
